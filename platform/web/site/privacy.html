Privacy notice

Butr

This page explains what Butr collects, why it collects it, and how it is handled.

What we collect

  • Contact details for signup and support
  • Trading 212 connection data if you link an account
  • Trade intents, previews, approvals, and audit events
  • Basic security logs such as timestamps and IP addresses

Why we collect it

  • To provide the Butr service
  • To preview trades and prepare execution-ready tickets
  • To secure accounts and investigate issues
  • To meet legal, regulatory, and audit obligations

Your rights

  • You can ask for access to your data
  • You can ask for correction or deletion where applicable
  • You can revoke broker access through Butr
  • You can ask questions about how your data is used
